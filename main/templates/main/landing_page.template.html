Our principles

Human-centered.
Education requires commitment, not better technology. Rocket focuses on helping you form better study habits and offers an environment tweaked to the last string for you. Designed by humans for humans.
Circle image
A cognitive approach.
Rocket takes advantage of the strengths and weaknesses of the human mind and exploits both to bolster learning. Allow us to manage the dedicated learning environment so that you can focus on your studies.

Education does not have a price tag. We at Rocket believe strongly in the equality of education for all. The Rocket team vows to never offer premium services or ask for any form of payment.
- Features -
(1) A break system.
One image here with picture of tired cartoon
Image of app's break system

You are not a machine, regardless of how much you might aspire to be one.
Rocket offers a simple, yet revolutionary break system that other learning applications do not consider.

Semi-optional breaks are offered throughout each course, allowing you to step outside, check your social media accounts, or enjoy a quick online video.

This is because we know studying can be difficult; headaches, fatigue, and cognitive exhaustion only make it worse. We promise to make it easier on you so that you can reach your learning goals.

(2) Multiple contexts.
image of reviews/pop-quiz

Your memory is better when you repeat things in your head.

Rocket offers a seamless review system that allows for repetition and re-encoding. This means more contexts for you to learn and understand course topics.

(3) Ask a "Tweetcher".
image of twitter

Yes, we came up with that pun.

It can be difficult to get help when you need it, be it due to availability or intimidation - we won't ask.

We integrated Twitter so that you can Tweet your questions anonymously to a teacher. This means quicker responses and less hesitation.
